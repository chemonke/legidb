EU 10/2011 plastics FCM database

This app wraps the Annex I and Annex III structures from Commission Regulation (EU) No 10/2011. Use it to explore authorised substances, food categories, simulants, and their migration limits.

Search the data See quick stats

What is inside?

  • Food categories and their reference numbers (Annex III).
  • Simulants linked to food categories.
  • Authorised substances with SML / SML(T) context.
  • REST API endpoints for programmatic access.

Purpose

Aligns daily compliance checks with the structure of EU 10/2011: quickly look up categories, simulants, and migration constraints when assessing materials.

Workflow

1) Load data into MariaDB; 2) Search by food category or name; 3) Pull JSON from the API; 4) Visualise coverage through the graphics tab.

Data provenance

Tables mirror Annex I (Table 1, 2) and Annex III structures and preserve raw expressions such as ND and unit basis. Seed data can be extended as your regulatory library grows.
